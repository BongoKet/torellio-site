TORELLIO — PRIVACY POLICY DRAFT
================================
Last updated: April 2026

---

1. WHO WE ARE

Torellio is an AI-powered shopping assistant currently in early development.
This privacy policy explains what personal data we collect through our website
(torellio.com or similar) and how we use it.

If you have any questions, contact us at: [YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS]

---

2. WHAT DATA WE COLLECT AND WHY

a) Waitlist sign-ups
   - Your email address
   - The date and time you signed up
   - The page you came from (referrer URL, if available)

   Why: So we can notify you when Torellio launches. Nothing else.

b) Feature ideas
   - Your idea and category
   - Your email address (optional — only if you choose to provide it)
   - The date and time of submission

   Why: To help us prioritise what to build. We may follow up if you left
   an email and we have questions about your idea.

---

3. HOW WE STORE YOUR DATA

Your data is stored securely in Cloudflare KV (key-value storage), hosted
on Cloudflare's infrastructure within their global network.

We do not use cookies. We do not use analytics tracking. We do not use
any third-party marketing or advertising tools.

---

4. WHO WE SHARE YOUR DATA WITH

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

The only sub-processor involved is Cloudflare, Inc., who stores the data
on our behalf. Cloudflare's privacy policy is available at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

---

5. HOW LONG WE KEEP YOUR DATA

Waitlist email addresses will be kept until:
  - We send the launch notification email, after which they will be deleted, OR
  - You ask us to remove you (see Section 6)

Feature ideas (without email) may be kept indefinitely as product research.
If you attached an email to an idea, that email will be deleted after we have
reviewed it or within 12 months, whichever is sooner.

---

6. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR)

If you are based in the UK or EU, you have the right to:

  - Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  - Deletion: Ask us to delete your data at any time
  - Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  - Restriction: Ask us to stop processing your data
  - Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  - Object: Object to us processing your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at: getintouch@bongosclub.uk
We will respond within 30 days.

---

7. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING (GDPR)

We process your email address on the basis of your consent — you actively
chose to join the waitlist. You can withdraw consent at any time by emailing
us and we will delete your data promptly.

---

8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the
top of this page. We will not retroactively change how we handle data
already collected without notifying you.

---

9. CONTACT

For any privacy-related queries or to exercise your rights:
Email: getintouch@bongosclub.uk

[OPTIONAL: If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you have the
right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)
at ico.org.uk.]
