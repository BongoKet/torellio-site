What Torellio does

Three tools. One smarter way to shop.

Smart comparison

Open product tabs in your browser, click compare. AI agents extract specs, research reviews, and deliver a clear verdict with the best price.

Visual scanner

Photograph a room and let AI identify the style, colours, and aesthetic — then find matching furniture and decor with links to buy.

Agent-powered research

Four AI agents work together — extracting data, researching alternatives, analysing tradeoffs, and giving you a personalised recommendation.

How it works

From open tabs to clear answers in seconds.

1

Browse as normal

Open product pages on any retailer. Torellio sits quietly in your browser until you need it.

2

Click compare

Hit the Torellio button. AI agents immediately start extracting specs and researching each product.

3

Agents do the work

They find reviews, check prices across retailers, and weigh up the tradeoffs — all in real time.

4

Get your verdict

A clear recommendation with reasoning, a spec comparison, and the best price with a direct link to buy.
