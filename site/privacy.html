Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who we are

Torellio is an AI-powered shopping assistant currently in early development. This policy explains what personal data we collect through our website and how we use it.

2. What data we collect and why

Waitlist sign-ups

  • Your email address
  • The date and time you signed up
  • The page you came from (referrer URL, if available)

We collect this solely to notify you when Torellio launches. Nothing else.

Feature ideas

  • Your idea and the category you selected
  • Your email address (optional — only if you choose to provide it)
  • The date and time of submission

We collect this to help us prioritise what to build. We may follow up if you left an email and we have questions about your idea.

3. How we store your data

Your data is stored securely in Cloudflare KV (key-value storage), hosted on Cloudflare's infrastructure. We do not use cookies, analytics tracking, or any third-party marketing tools.

4. Who we share your data with

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. The only sub-processor involved is Cloudflare, Inc., who stores the data on our behalf. Their privacy policy is available at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

5. How long we keep your data

Waitlist email addresses are kept until we send the launch notification, after which they will be deleted — or sooner if you ask us to remove you.

Feature ideas (without email) may be kept indefinitely as product research. If you attached an email to an idea, that email will be deleted after we review it or within 12 months, whichever is sooner.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are based in the UK or EU, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your data at any time
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Restriction — ask us to stop processing your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object — object to us processing your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at getintouch@bongosclub.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

We process your email address on the basis of your consent — you actively chose to join the waitlist. You can withdraw consent at any time by emailing us and we will delete your data promptly.

8. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. We will not retroactively change how we handle data already collected without notifying you.

Questions about your data? Email getintouch@bongosclub.uk — we'll respond within 30 days. If you're unhappy with how we handle your data, you can also lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).
